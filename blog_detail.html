Call to Teach

Summary: Correct sizing prevents cavitation, improves stability, and lowers lifecycle cost. This quick guide covers service conditions, flow/ΔP checks, and material choices.

Define service conditions

Start with medium, temperature, operating pressure, and shutoff class. For treated water, EPDM seats are common; for chemicals or higher temperatures consider NBR, Viton, or PTFE.

Check flow & pressure drop

Use line size as a starting point, then verify with Kv/Cv. Aim for 30–70% open at normal operation.

  • Medium: Chilled/process water, air
  • Pressure: PN10–PN16 typical
  • Mounting: ISO 5211 top flange
